About the Designer

Hi, I am Marci the owner and the designer of this website. I am currently going to school full-time at BYU-Idaho and majoring in Graphic and Web Design. I would describe myself as a motivated, athletic, and artistic. My art ability has allowed me to become a creative Graphic and Web Designer. As for my athletic ability, my performances have trained me to stick to a task and to always work hard no matter what. I am currently married to my husband, Ben, who is a big supporter of my work and hobbies. We currently live in Utah and we will soon be moving to the Bay Area. There we hope to explore new opportunities.


About the Website

This website will be used to share my work and hobbies within the art boundaries of a Graphic and Web Design. My Design page will show you the finished projects of all my works with a brief description of each piece of work. Here I hope to show my best work in the Web and Graphic Design field. My page labeled as Art will show you the rough sketches and processes of creating a final piece of work. You can also find some finished paintings and drawings as well. The Art page will mainly be used for unfinished works and fun side hobbies.

I hope you can find something that you can enjoy at a professional and as a inspirational level. There is something here for everyone.
