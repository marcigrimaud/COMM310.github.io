Rhino Sports

This is one of the very first Logos that I made when and it is one of the reasons why I love designing. To make this logo I had to take 2 completely different things and turn it into one. I picked Animals and Clothing for the inspiration for my logo. I choose the Rhino because I liked how tough and strong they were. After I choose that I started thinking "what type of clothing needs to be tough and strong?" And that is when I got the idea for Rhino Sports to be a clothing store.
